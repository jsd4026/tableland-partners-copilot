## Setup (15 Minutes)

### Step 1: Create Your Claude Account

Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) and sign up (or log in if you have an account).

### Step 2: Create a New Project

1. Click **Projects** at the top of your screen
2. Click **Create Project**
3. Name it: `[Your Business Name] - Tableland Copilot`

### Step 3: Add the Project Description

Copy and paste this into the **Project Description** field:

```
Tableland Copilot is your AI-powered business assistant that guides you step-by-step through building a complete business foundation—from strategy and branding to go-to-market execution, tech stack setup, expense tracking, and field team support. This system helps you create professional business documents, develop marketing strategies, configure tools and integrations, track expenses, support field employees, and launch successfully—even if you're starting from scratch. Designed for small business owners, consultants, and entrepreneurs who want to build a sustainable, profitable business with AI-powered support for daily operations.
```

### Step 4: Add the Project Instructions

Copy the **entire contents** of [`setup-mode-instructions.md`](docs/setup-mode-instructions.md) and paste it into the **Custom Instructions** (or **Instructions**) field in your project settings.

These instructions tell the AI how to guide you through the complete setup process.

### Step 5: Upload the Implementation Guide

1. Download [`Complete_Implementation_Guide_v4.docx`](docs/Complete_Implementation_Guide_v4.docx) from this repo
2. In your Claude Project, find **Knowledge** or **Files**
3. Click **Add files** or **Upload**
4. Select the guide you just downloaded

> This is the "brain" — it contains every prompt, every phase, every checkpoint, and every document template the AI needs to build your business foundation.

### Step 6: Enable Conversation Search

1. Click your **profile icon** (top right) → **Settings**
2. Find **"Search and reference past chats"** and turn it **ON**
3. This allows the AI to reference work from other conversations in your project

### Step 7: Start Your First Conversation

1. Go back to your project and start a **new conversation**
2. Copy the **entire contents** of [`conversation-0-prompt.md`](docs/conversation-0-prompt.md) and paste it as your first message
3. Hit **Enter**
4. The AI will ask you to rename the conversation to `0: Copilot Setup` — follow its instructions
5. Then it will start asking about your business

**That's it. You're running.**

The AI guides you through everything from here. It will tell you when to create each new conversation, what to paste, and when you've hit each quality checkpoint.

## How It Works

The system uses **10 conversations** (think of them as specialized departments), created progressively as you complete each phase:

```
Phase 0: Discovery
  └── 0: Copilot Setup — Tell the AI about your business

Phase 1: Foundation (11 documents)
  └── 1: Strategic Planning & Business Foundation

Phase 2: Go-to-Market (5 documents)
  └── 2: Go-to-Market Strategy & Execution

Phase 3: Operations (5 documents)
  ├── 3: Technical Infrastructure & Automation
  └── 4: Customer Experience

Phase 4: Execution (ongoing daily use)
  ├── 5: Content Creation & Marketing Assets
  ├── 6: Proposals and Agreements
  ├── 7: Prospecting & Lead Generation
  ├── 8: Receipt Capture & Expense Tracking
  └── 9: Field Support Agent
```

After completing all phases, you switch from **Setup Mode** to **Operational Mode** and the Copilot becomes your daily business operating system.

## What Happens After Setup

Once you complete the guided setup (2–4 weeks), you update the project instructions to **Operational Mode**. The AI switches from "build your business" to "run your business" — and your 6 agents are ready for daily use.

**Your daily workflow might look like:**
- **Monday:** Open Prospecting Agent → generate 5 qualified leads with pitches
- **Tuesday:** Open Marketing Agent → batch a week of LinkedIn content
- **Wednesday:** Open Proposals Agent → draft 2 proposals from voice notes
- **Thursday:** Open Field Support → your team gets instant technical answers
- **Friday:** Open Finance Agent → snap and process the week's receipts

## FAQ

**Is this really free?**
Yes. The tool, the guide, and the setup instructions are 100% free. You just need a Claude account (free tier works, Pro recommended). If you want live support, coaching, and a community of business owners using these tools, join the [AI Essentials Roundtable](https://tablelandpartners.com/ai_essentials_roundtable/) ($97/month, first month free).

**How long does setup take?**
The initial project creation takes 15 minutes. The full guided build (all documents, all agents) takes 2–4 weeks at your own pace, working through it in conversations.

**Do I need to know how to code?**
No. You are having conversations with the AI. It does the technical work.

**Can I use this with Grok or Jasper instead of Claude?**
Yes. The Implementation Guide and conversation prompts work with any AI that supports projects or custom instructions. Claude is recommended because the system was built and tested on it.

**What if I get stuck?**
Say **"I need Jeff's help"** in any conversation. The AI will generate a share link and draft an email to Jeffrey Daniels (jeff@tablelandpartners.com). For ongoing support, join the [AI Essentials Roundtable](https://tablelandpartners.com/ai_essentials_roundtable/).

**Can I customize the agents?**
Absolutely. The system is built so you can add new conversations for any purpose — client-specific projects, competitive intelligence, hiring, you name it. The Implementation Guide shows you how.

**Can I fork this and modify it?**
Yes. MIT License. Fork it, customize it, make it yours.
